About

Yanet Gomez is a data analyst at Cherokee Federal since October 2021. Prior to completing her MSDA at UHD, she worked in Financial Management and Information Management for more than 10 years in the United States Air Force (USAF). She holds bachelors degrees in Anthropology & Sociology as well as Integrated Communications from Florida International University. She currently leads analytics projects in support of the Air Force Medical Readiness Agency (AFMRA). Her strengths include wrangling and analyzing data in R and Python, designing impactful dashboards in Tableau, and managing teams and projects from development to sustainment. She values accuracy and function over speed and flashiness, and has a keen interest in design principles. When she is not cleaning data in R, running Tableau Prep flows, or designing dashboards, you can find her traveling (or planning a trip).
